Photo Resize

Photo Resize is a simple sharing extension for Android which helps you send photos via email or messaging more quickly. It creates a temporary smaller version of your photo before you send it so that you don’t have to send the full couple of megabytes of a 8MP photo via email.

You can find and follow Photo Resize on Google+.
